# Privacy Policy for E-Kicevo

**Last Updated:** November 23, 2025

## Introduction
E-Kicevo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our municipal problem reporting application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Phone Number**: Used for authentication and account verification
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates when submitting reports
- **Photos**: Images you upload when creating reports

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage data
- Crash reports and error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Authenticate and verify user accounts
- Process and track municipal problem reports
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Communicate updates about your reports

## Data Storage

- User data is stored securely on Supabase servers
- Photos are uploaded to our backend server hosted on Render.com
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell or share your personal information with third parties except:
- With municipal authorities to process your reports
- When required by law or legal process

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt-out of location tracking (though this limits report submission)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@ekicevo.com

## Children's Privacy

E-Kicevo is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **Google Gemini AI**: Image analysis
- **Render.com**: Backend hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data protection.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:
- Email: support@ekicevo.com
- Address: Municipality of Kicevo, North Macedonia

## Consent

By using E-Kicevo, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
