Privacy Policy for E-Kicevo

Last updated: November 21, 2025

The Municipality of Kicevo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our E-Kicevo mobile application collects, uses, and discloses your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect your precise location (GPS) when you submit a report to identify the problem area. We also use location to show nearby parking zones.
  • Camera & Photos: We access your camera and photo library to allow you to attach images to your reports.
  • User Account: We collect your phone number and authentication details to manage your account and report history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and resolve municipal problems (potholes, trash, etc.).
  • Process parking payments.
  • Send you status updates regarding your reports.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in our database (Supabase) and is only accessible by authorized municipal staff.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@kicevo.gov.mk.
